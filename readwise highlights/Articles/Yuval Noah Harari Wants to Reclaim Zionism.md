- Summary: Yuval Noah Harari argues that Zionism is often misunderstood and should be reclaimed as a legitimate expression of Jewish identity and rights. He emphasizes that both Jews and Palestinians have the right to self-determination and that acknowledging one does not negate the other. Harari also critiques Israeli leadership, suggesting that unity is essential for addressing the country's existential threats.
